Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Founded on October 27th, 2017 by young entrepreneur Farhan Chowdhury Mahir, ZuperMart has come a long way.

The owner Farhan himself is a fashion enthusiast. All the clothing, accessories are handpicked and designed to always suit the current trend. In your busy lives, keeping you effortlessly stylish is our mission. You don’t need to go to various shops, you will find all your needs here. Shirts, T-shirts, Pants, Shoes, Accessories and more.

Unlike any other, we are always following our customer needs and keeping track of their satisfaction through the large community we have built.

Founder

Farhan Chowdhury Mahir

A fashion enthusiast himself who always dreamt about being in the fashion industry. From a young age did modelling for various brands and developed a kin sense of fashion himself. And at a young age of 19, he opened the shop he always dreamt about which reflected sharp and trendy fashion sense that he has.

ZuperMart is not just a store, it’s the passion of someone who loves fashion.
